SMART CITY CONCEPT

For this project, we were required to come up with an innovative concept for a smart city. Ours was 'Blankey': an app that connects to machines holding blankets around parks, and through the app you can borrow a blanket fro the machine and use it in the park. Even though it is quite a simple idea, it received quite a good response from our testers.
The website is a mobile prototype for the app, with a sign-up/login system for the users. Coded with a focus on the PHP.
